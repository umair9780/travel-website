Work Permit Visas

Temporary Work Visa

Duration: Up to 6 months

Description: This visa is for individuals who wish to work temporarily in a foreign country. It is usually tied to a specific job and employer.

Skilled Worker Visa

Duration: Up to 5 years

Description: This visa is for skilled workers who have a job offer in a foreign country and meet the specific skills and qualification requirements.

Permanent Work Visa

Duration: Permanent

Description: This visa allows individuals to live and work permanently in a foreign country. It is typically based on having a permanent job offer or meeting certain residency requirements.
